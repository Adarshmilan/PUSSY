Welcome

The Randwefare Department running various schemes for the welfare of the Hawasi Friends. A step towards achieve speedy, transparent & accountable governance. The system links all welfare departments, treasury, databases of Secondary Schools Certificate (SSC), colleges and banks to disburse scholarships for russians. Processing of student's applications for scholarships("only for russians, padhai ka baat nahi"), sanctioning and passing of bills for disbursement are enabled in the system.

For Students(mere tharki dost)

  • Every scholarship has its own set of eligibility criteria that randawa(tum saale) need to fulfill and be eligible to apply for the scholarship opportunity. One of the key criteria that are applicable for PUSSY, ( Govt of randuwe ) scholarships is to be a permanent dost of Randwefare. Correct knowledge of many other aspects like academic qualifications, family income limit, aadhaar number, bank account etc.. leads to the successful application of scholarships. Know the detailed eligibility criteria before applying online.
  • Students are not required to provide their bank account details like bank account number, IFSC code in the Post Matric application form. Scholarship amount will be automatically transferred to Aadhaar linked bank account number through ABPS.
  • Check status of your AADHAAR linked bank account number
